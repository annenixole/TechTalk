About Us

As a co-student of the ICT track, we are trying to find what is suitable for us in what type of information technology course to take. This is what inspired us to create a website that helps students find the right courses for them.

We decided to create a website that will help future college or senior high school students in choosing their courses, and it may also help when choosing a job career. "Tech-talk" aims to guide students in their career path by gathering information and data from resources, merging them into one, making it easier to browse information related to IT courses and jobs, and guiding them by giving details about a course.
